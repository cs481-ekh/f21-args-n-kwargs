The instruments listed in this database provide a large range of capabilities for synthesis, characterization, and processing of materials of all types. Whether the instrumentation is managed through a federal recharge center or an individual faculty lab, the resources of the School are available to advance your materials research and development needs. Contact information is provided for each instrument, but if you don't find what you need, please contact the School at mseequipment@boisestate.edu. Database registration provides full access to the database for Boise State University students, faculty, and staff
